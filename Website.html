WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF GIVING!

This is a website that demonstrates an application I would like to develop in the future. It's solely to help people through donations. There's food that goes to waste everyday but is not used. I would like to make use of the same in order to satisfy hunger of the poor and needy. Every single one of you can contribute, and make a difference in the lives of many people.


Here's how it works:

  1. The application will have a "sign up" for those interested in taking part.
  2. Once signed up, a scheduled meeting will be held with the company I run, for official reasons, and also in order to particularly meet each giving client to share our token of appreciation.
  3. Any company or even an individual is most welcome.
  4. Once a contract is set between both parties, it works easy.
  5. For each time there is a minimal amount of food, a food company wants to share, even an individual, there will be a quick and easy "alert" signal that asks the name of the company or person, and what and how much food they hold at this point.
  6. Once our company is notified, and the client is verified, a food truck will be sent to collect the food.
  7. This food will be delivered to those found on streets, homeless and the needy. Shelters for the homeless, and organisations that help such people, will be given a part of the share in order to serve the hungry.
